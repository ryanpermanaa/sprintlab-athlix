About Venue

Experience the vibrant spirit of basketball in paradise at Metro Basketball Court, Bali. Located in a strategic area with easy access, this modern court offers the perfect blend of professional facilities and tropical atmosphere. Whether you’re here for a casual game with friends, a competitive match, or a training session, Metro Basketball Court is designed to deliver top performance and comfort.

With a full-sized hardwood court, high-quality hoops, bright LED lighting for evening games, and spacious seating for spectators, it’s the ultimate spot for basketball lovers. Plus, on-site amenities such as locker rooms, parking, and a refreshment corner ensure a hassle-free experience.
