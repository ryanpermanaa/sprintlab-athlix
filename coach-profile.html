Short Bio

Coach Mike is a certified basketball trainer with over 7 years of experience coaching both youth and professional players. Known for his motivational approach and focus on teamwork, he helps athletes improve both skill and mindset.

Experience

  • Over 8 years of coaching experience in basketball training.
  • Former assistant coach for Bali Eagles Basketball Club (2018–2022).
  • Head coach for several junior development programs and school tournaments.
  • Has successfully trained 100+ athletes, including regional-level players.

Coaching

Coach Mike focuses on helping players build strong fundamentals while developing advanced game awareness and teamwork. His training sessions are designed to be dynamic, practical, and motivating, suitable for both beginners and competitive athletes.

Specialties include:

  • Shooting accuracy & form correction

  • Dribbling control & ball-handling drills

  • Speed, agility, and footwork improvement

  • Offensive & defensive strategies

  • Team coordination & communication

  • Strength and endurance conditioning

  • Game analysis and performance review
